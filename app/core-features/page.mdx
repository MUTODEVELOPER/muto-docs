# Core Features

## 1. MUTO AI Agent
The flagship utility layer of the ecosystem. MUTO AI is being developed as a practical crypto assistant with on-chain identity and analytics capabilities.

## 2. Analytics-first direction
MUTO is focused on information utility. The ecosystem is intended to offer easier access to token data, wallet context, and market signals.

## 3. Builder culture
MUTO is not framed as a passive meme project. Its social identity is grounded in shipping, iteration, and community participation.

## 4. Multi-surface presence
The project is designed to live across web, Telegram, and future integrated tools.

## 5. Expandable ecosystem
MUTO is built with room for additional products, dashboards, research tools, and AI modules.
